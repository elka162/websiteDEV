About Kongur Warriors

Kongur Warriors Dashboard is an online platform designed to inspire and unite the Kongur Warriors community, associated with Holy Cross Church in Kulshekar, Mangalore. This site highlights achievements from the unity of various wards, including recent victories in sports meets, fostering pride and camaraderie among members. Established to strengthen community bonds, it showcases success stories that resonate with all, making it a favorite for those looking to celebrate teamwork and accomplishment. By focusing on the triumphs of 2023 and 2024's church sports meets, it serves as a vibrant digital hub for inspiration and connection.
